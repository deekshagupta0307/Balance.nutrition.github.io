About

This website is a valuable resource for individuals seeking to improve their health and well-being through diet and lifestyle changes. One of the primary benefits of the website is that it can provide evidence-based information and resources to help people make informed decisions about their health. The website offers interactive tools and resources to help users track their progress, set goals, and stay motivated. For example, users may be able to log their daily food intake and physical activity on the Heath Quiz, monitor their weight and body mass index (BMI), will be able to monitor calorie intake to gain/maintain/loose weight.
